# Advancements

The Server provides its own sets of custom Advancements which can be unlocked while playing on the game.

## Criterias

Depending on the Advancement are you required to fulfill specific criterias. As an example, the "Take a seat" advancement requires you to sit on any furniture, that is sittable.  
Many advancements simply require you to obtain the item they are connected to. If an item is unlocking a specific Advancement, will their wiki page contain a "Advancements" section for you to read.
