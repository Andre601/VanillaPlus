# Totem of Experience

{{ infobox("vanillaplus:totem_of_experience") }}

**Totem of Experience** is an item that upon a player's death, stores their XP, allowing it to be retrieved to regain all their lost XP.

## Usage

The totem activates automatically while in the Player's inventory upon their death.  
When this happens does the Totem change its texture to indicate it being used. Additionally will Enchanting Table particles show around it while in dropped Item form.

When right-clicking the totem while it is filled, will all the stored XP be returned to the player. This will display the Totem Animation while also consuming the totem itself.

## Obtaining

There is currently no way of obtaining this item.
